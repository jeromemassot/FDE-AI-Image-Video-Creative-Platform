# Creation of an image from a text
Here is a short checklist for creating an 8-second video from a text description using Veo3:

## 1. Prompt Design
Description: Write a detailed and vivid text prompt. Specify the subject, action, setting, camera angle, and desired visual style (e.g., cinematic, photorealistic, 4K).

## 2. Video Generation
Description: Click the "Generate Video" button after having selected the Veo3 model that you want to use.

## 3. Review & Iterate
Description: Carefully watch the generated video for visual quality, accuracy to the prompt, and motion consistency. If it's not perfect, refine your prompt with more specific details or negative prompts and regenerate.

## 4. Finalize & Export
Description: Once you are satisfied with a generated video, download it on your computer from the embedded video player.
